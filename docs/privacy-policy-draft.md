# Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: 2026-06-22

This is a draft for review before public release.

## App

Battle of the Bands lets friends create shared music battle rooms, add Spotify albums or artists, play music through Spotify links, and vote for winners.

## Information The App May Process

The app may process:

- display names entered by room members
- room codes and invite links
- music picks added to a room
- Spotify search queries
- Spotify album, artist, artwork, and link metadata
- votes, battle results, and battle history

## How Information Is Used

Information is used to:

- create and join battle rooms
- show the shared music list to room members
- run battles and count votes
- show battle history and winners
- open Spotify links for playback

## Third-Party Services

The app may use:

- Supabase for shared room data
- Spotify for music search metadata and playback links
- Expo services for development, builds, and app delivery

Spotify playback and Spotify account behavior are handled by Spotify.

## Data Retention

Room data remains available while stored in the configured backend. Before public release, define the retention period and deletion process for rooms, votes, and battle history.
